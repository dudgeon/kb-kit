# decision

Capture decisions where they happened, not in slide decks. Frontmatter should
carry `date` and, when known, `owner`. Body: context, the decision, options
rejected and why, and links to affected pages. Decisions are immutable once
made — a reversal is a new decision page linking back with "supersedes".
